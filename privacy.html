COOKIES


What are cookies?


A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer when you browse it. Thanks to this file, the website stores information about your actions and preferences for a certain time, so that the next time you visit the website, the browser reloads the cookies and sends this information back to the website that originally created the cookie. The cookies we use on our website do not harm your computer.


We may ask to process cookies on your device. We may use cookies to keep us informed when you visit our website, how you interact with us, and to enrich the user experience and customize your experience with our website.


By clicking on the individual items of the cookies category you can learn more about its processing. You can also change your preferences. Blocking some types of cookies may affect your experience with our websites and the services we can offer.


We use cookies to help you navigate efficiently and perform certain functions. You will find detailed information about all cookies under each consent category below.


Necessary cookies:


The cookies that are categorized as "Necessary" are stored on your browser as they are essential for enabling the basic functionalities of the site. These cookies are absolutely essential to provide the services available on our website and to use some of its functions. As these cookies are absolutely necessary to deliver the website, rejecting them would affect how our site works.


Other than necessary cookies:


We also use third-party cookies that help us analyze how you use this website, store your preferences, and provide the content and advertisements that are relevant to you. These cookies will only be stored in your browser with your prior consent. We provide you with a list of cookies stored on your computer in our domain so that you can check what we have stored. You can choose to enable or disable some or all of these cookies but disabling some of them may affect your browsing experience.


Google Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information that is used either in aggregate form to help us understand how our website is used or how effective our marketing campaigns are, or to help us customize our website and app for you to improve your experience. If you do not want us to track your visit to our website, you can turn off the processing of this type of cookie.


Other external services: We may also use external services Google reCaptcha, Google, Microsoft, Microsoft Advertising, Microsoft Clarity, Bing, LinkedIn, Youtube, Instagram, Twitter, tawk.to inc., GitHub, medium.com and/or external video providers. Because these providers may collect personal data, such as your IP address, we allow you to block them. However, blocking may significantly reduce the functionality and appearance of our site.
